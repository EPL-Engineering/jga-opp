Audio mixer

 

 

 

 

The audio mixer control allows the tester to set independently the level of the "background" stimulus sent to the Caregiver and Waver. It also provides control of the talkback from the Tester to the Waver via the USB microphone. The talkback can be muted here, but can also be toggled by pressing the space bar from the OPP main panel.

 

The right-most slider provides control of the Waver-to-Tester communication over the in-booth USB microphone.

 

Finally, the audio mixer provides control of the level of the audio accompanying the video reward presented to the participant.

 

Importantly, note that there is no control over the level of the stimulus presented to the participant: this is calibrated in software to achieve the SPLs specified as part of the experiment.
