Checking the audio

 

 

 

 

To adjust the audio levels at the beginning of an experiment, select Tools > Audio > Test from the main menu

 

The following dialog box will appear. Press Play to begin streaming the signals to the Caregiver, Waver, and Participant that they will hear in the currently selected Phase of the OPP experiment. The content of each of these signals is indicated on the dialog (e.g.: the Caregiver and Waver each hear a 782-Hz masker at 30 dB followed by a 1257-Hz probe tone at 70 dB. Only the masker is delivered to the Participant channel, but the masker + probe stimulus can be tested by pressing the "Do it" button.

 

 

 

The levels of the sounds delivered to the Caregiver and Waver can now be adjust using the audio mixer.

 

 

Bonus troubleshooting feature

 

It is possible for the investigator to listen to the signals by connecting a pair of headphones to the F output on the front of the MOTU Monitor 8, and clicking the box next to the signal they wish to hear. For example, by clicking the "Caregiver" box, the audio sent to the Caregiver can be heard on output F. This feature works only on the Audio Test dialog box.
