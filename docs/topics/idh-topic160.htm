Training phase

 

 

 

 

 

Block size

In this training example, the block size is 8 and there are 6 signals (i.e.: probe trials) per block.

Reward does not require response

In the training phase, this option is unchecked, meaning that the reward (toys or video) is presented on every signal trial (i.e.: probe) whether the Participant responds or not

Pass condition

The training phase ends successfully if the Participant withholds (does not respond) to 1 of the last no-probe trials AND responds to 4 of the last 5 probe trials

Max number of trials

 

Stop condition

The training phase ends unsuccessfully if the max number of trials (i.e.: 20) has been reached without achieving the pass condition. In this case, the experiment just stops, because there is no where to bounce back to.
