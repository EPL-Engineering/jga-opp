Testing the video

 

 

 

 

Video playback can be tested by selecting Tools > Video > Test from the main menu

 

 

This will bring up the following dialog, from where the video can be started and stopped. The video can also be toggled by pressing the 4 key from the OPP main panel. Use the drop down menu to select one of the videos in the playlist created in the Settings dialog.
