Testing a participant with a Bluetooth device

 

OPP testing with a Bluetooth device requires a simple change to the Windows audio settings. Specifically, the Bluetooth device must "listen" to a copy of the participant signal sent from the MOTU device back to the PC.

 

To minimize the need to burrow through Windows settings to find the right dialog box, there is a shortcut on the Acoustic PTC panel. It appears when the presentation mode involves a Bluetooth device and the OPP method is selected. It is probably best to complete the Bluetooth pairing before following the steps below.

 

 

Clicking the setup button brings up the Recording tab on the Windows Sound settings dialog.

•Double-click the "In 1-2" MOTU device

 

 

•then click the "Listen" tab and

1.Check "Listen to this device"

2.Select the paired Bluetooth device from the dropdown menu
