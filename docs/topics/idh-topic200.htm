Checking engagement with a training stimulus

 

 

 

 

During testing, if it becomes unclear whether the Participant is unable to detect the signal or has simply become disengaged from the task, it is possible to present a training (i.e. easily-detectable) stimulus. Press the "T" key on the keyboard to revert to the training stimulus. Text will appear on the main panel as shown below to indicate that the application is in this state. The text is initially dim and turns bright when the audio streamer is ready to play the training stimulus.

 

Pressing the "Enter" key with the training stimulus activated will always play a signal. The result of the trial is displayed on screen and entered into the log, but is not otherwise recorded.

 

Press the "T" key again to toggle back to the test stimulus.
