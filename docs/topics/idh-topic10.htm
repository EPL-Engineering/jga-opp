Getting started

 

The experiment starts using the Acoustic PTC app in the usual way. The Method control in the lower left (1) is new, and allows you to select between "Standard" (the usual gui-based alternative forced choice) or "OPP". Selecting "OPP" hides the AFC tracking details that normally appear on the right. Pressing START brings up the OPP app itself.

 

The settings on the Acoustic PTC main panel are transferred to the OPP, e.g: probe/masker selections, levels and timing properties.

 

Note that the example shown here is used throughout this documentation: the experiment is to fix the masker level and vary the probe level.
