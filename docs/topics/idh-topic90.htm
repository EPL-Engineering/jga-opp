Running the experiment

 

 

 

 

Begin the experiment by selecting the desired Phase and pressing Start. The audio will begin streaming to the Caregiver, Waver, and Participant.

 

 

The structure of a trial is illustrated here. The Participant hears an ongoing stream of maskers. When the Participant is suitably attentive to begin an actual trial, the Tester presses the Enter key. When the masker + probe combination begins to play, the OPP main panel will turn red (as shown below) for the duration specified in the Settings dialog. This corresponds to the time window during which the Tester can press the + key to indicate that the Participant has responded to the presence of the probe stimulus.
